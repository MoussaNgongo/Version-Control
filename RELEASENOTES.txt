Introducing the Bard class!
Explore the realm as a wandering musician,
regaling patrons in taverns with your captivating stories and enchanting music.
Choose from a wide array of bizarre and fantastical tales to entertain your audience,
from legends of ancient dragons to whispered secrets of hidden treasures.
But beware - not all who listen have pure intentions,
and danger may lurk in the shadows.
So sharpen your wit and hone your skills,
for the life of a bard is full of adventure and peril!
